Basic Personal Information Collection, Processing, or Use
ROE will store and maintain the basic personal information you provide voluntarily to maintain the communication between you and ROE, such as sending information, newsletters, or e-mails, until you raise a request to ROE for the termination of the storage directly or until the law requires termination of the storage of said information. Without prior consent, ROE will never disclose, sell, or give your personal information to other irrelevant organizations for marketing or sales.
