9. How long do we store your Personal Information
We will retain your Personal Information for the period necessary to fulfil the purposes for which your Personal Information has been collected as outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required by law. When your Personal Information is no longer required for the purpose it was collected or as required by applicable law, it will be deleted in accordance with applicable law. We will also by your request delete your Information collected by NUVOTON, you may contact us to process such request.
